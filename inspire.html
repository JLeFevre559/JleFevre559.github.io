Charlie Brown and Franz Stigler

Portrait

What makes these two heroes?

During WW2 Charlie Brown was on a bombing run in a B-17F, when his bomber was severely damaged by German fighters

Franz Stigler was in a perfect position to shoot Charlie Brown's bomber down, but instead he chose to escort his bomber to protect it from being shot down.

This story proves that even through the horrors of war, humanity and care for other humans can come through and win the day.

50 years after the war, the two pilots found each other and developed a friendship that lasted until death.
